ADA Title II — Key Digital Obligations for State & Local Agencies
What government agencies are legally required to address under updated DOJ ADA Title II rules (effective 2026)
✅
WCAG 2.1 Level AA Conformance
All public-facing web content and mobile apps must meet WCAG 2.1 AA — the baseline standard under DOJ's final rule.
✅
Compliance Deadline — April 2026 (large agencies) / 2027 (small)
DOJ final rule sets phased deadlines. Large jurisdictions (pop. 50,000+) must comply by April 24, 2026.
⚠️
Scope: All Web & Mobile Properties
Rule covers websites, mobile apps, web-based documents, and third-party platforms used to deliver government services.
⚠️
PDF & Document Accessibility
All publicly available PDFs, forms, and downloadable documents must be tagged and accessible — a common gap in current agency estates.
🔴
Non-Compliance Risk
Failure to comply exposes agencies to DOJ complaints, civil rights litigation, and loss of federal funding — making remediation a legal priority, not just a best practice.
