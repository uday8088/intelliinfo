Healthcare — Software Development Services

Healthcare includes any field of operation associated with the improvement of human health. Major fields include medical, hospital, and dental practice activities, as well as the work of physiotherapists, nurses, and diagnostic laboratories. The industry also spans health care services and equipment, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and life sciences. Intelli Infotech serves all of these healthcare industry fields, with the exception of embedded programming of medical equipment.

Electronic Health / Medical Record (EHR / EMR)

- Systematic collection of electronic health information about individual patients or populations
- Shareable across different healthcare settings via information system networks spanning entire organisations
- Covers demographics, medical history, medication and allergies, immunisation status, laboratory test results, radiology images, vital signs, personal stats (age, weight), and billing information
- Reporting functionality

Medical Practice Management Software

- Day-to-day operations management for inpatient, outpatient, long-term care, and rehabilitation providers
- Patient demographics recording
- Appointment scheduling and referral management
- Insurance provider lists management
- Billing tasks and financial tracking
- Report generation
