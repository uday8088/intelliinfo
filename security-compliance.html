Our Security Practice

How We Protect Client Data & Engagements

🔒
Confidentiality by Contract
Every engagement begins with a mutual NDA. Client data, IP, and programme details are never shared without written consent.
🛡️
Secure Access Controls
MFA enforced on all client-facing tools. No shared credentials. Separate access credentials per client environment.
☁️
Cloud-Hosted Work Products
Deliverables stored in client-owned or approved cloud environments (SharePoint, Google Drive, Azure) — not on personal devices.
👁️
Supervised Offshore Activity
Any India-based delivery is supervised by a US-based principal. No unsupervised access to sensitive client systems or PII.
📋
Engagement-Specific Security Plans
For regulated industries, we prepare a lightweight security plan defining data classification, handling, and retention for that programme.
🗑️
Data Disposal Policy
Client data is returned or securely destroyed at engagement close. No retention of client information beyond agreed periods.
