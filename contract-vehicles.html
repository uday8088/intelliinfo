Engagement Models

Available Contract Types

T&M
Time & Materials
Best for evolving scope or staff augmentation. Billed on actual hours and materials at agreed rates.
Maximum flexibility for changing requirements
Transparent hourly rate schedule
Monthly invoicing with detailed timesheets
Ideal for long-term staff augmentation
FFP
Firm Fixed Price
Best for well-defined deliverables. Fixed price for a defined scope — predictable cost, clear accountability.
Budget certainty from day one
Milestone-based payment schedule
Defined deliverables and acceptance criteria
Ideal for audits, assessments, and projects
Retainer
Monthly Retainer
Best for ongoing advisory, fractional PMO, or continuous accessibility monitoring. Predictable monthly commitment.
Reserved capacity each month
Ongoing advisory and escalation support
Discounted effective rate vs. T&M
Ideal for fractional leadership roles
