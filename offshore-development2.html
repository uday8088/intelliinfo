Offshore Development Center

Every year an increasing number of IT companies choose to outsource some of their work to remote specialists — either outsourcing the development of separate projects, or establishing their own Offshore Development Center (ODC) that serves as an extension of their development team.

The work of large IT companies primarily consists of the constant development of new products as well as regular software updates and maintenance. By establishing an ODC, a company creates its own virtual office with development staff exclusively assigned to that company's needs, working according to its requirements and standards.

Intelli Infotech has been offering high-quality ODC services since 2003. Our experience shows that long-term engagements such as ODC services can be a cost-effective and time-saving solution for all parties involved.
