We Offer Two Payment Models

Hourly Time & Materials

A flexible model which allows you to change your mind frequently. It does not require in-depth planning of project details up front and is used most often amongst our clients. For projects based on this model you can typically expect results within days.

Fixed Price

A model which gives you the security of a fixed price for the project. It generally requires extensive planning up front so that we can provide a realistic estimate. Fixed-price projects often cost more than hourly projects, since we assume the project risk.
