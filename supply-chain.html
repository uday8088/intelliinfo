Supply Chain Digital Maturity — Progression Model
We assess current state and build a roadmap to Level 4 or 5
Level 1
Manual & Fragmented
Paper-based POs, spreadsheet tracking, no supplier integration. High error rate and zero real-time visibility.
Level 2
Digitised Transactions
Basic ERP/procurement system in place. Electronic POs but siloed from inventory and logistics data.
Level 3
Connected & Integrated
Supplier portals integrated with ERP. API-driven data exchange. Beginning of real-time tracking.
Level 4
Predictive & Data-Driven
AI-powered demand forecasting, disruption alerts, and automated replenishment. Near real-time end-to-end visibility.
Level 5
Autonomous & Resilient
Self-optimising networks. AI-driven sourcing decisions. Continuous SCRM monitoring and adaptive response.
